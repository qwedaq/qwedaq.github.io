# Content provenance and accuracy notes

The site content was assembled from four explicit sources:

1. The supplied 2026 CV/resume, used for the current role, research experience,
   education, honors, selected recent publications, public profile links, and CV.
2. The previous Google Sites Home page, used for the early research narrative
   and historical news archive.
3. The previous Google Sites Community Service and Publications pages, used for
   reviewer venues, teaching records, and publications not listed in the
   selected-publication section of the CV.
4. Direct instructions supplied during the website-design discussion, including
   the research framework, teaching-assistant roles, official 2022 workshop
   title, `MM/YYYY` news format, and personal gratitude note.

## Deliberate safeguards

- No citation count or h-index value is manually invented. Until the first
  successful Scholar synchronization, these metrics display an em dash.
- The local publication list is a verified seed, not a claim that it is the
  current complete Scholar record. The first manual Scholar workflow imports
  the full current author-profile result set.
- Scholar records are deduplicated primarily using normalized titles and
  secondarily using DOI values when available.
- News items were not assigned invented acceptance dates. New CV-derived entries
  are included only where the CV provides a month and year.
- Reviewer years and journal impact factors were intentionally removed.
- The attached headshot was not available in the active artifact workspace.
  The repository therefore contains an explicit initials placeholder, with one
  documented file replacement needed to use the real portrait.
